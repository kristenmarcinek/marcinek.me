Meditative Mosaics

Meditative Mosaics are felt pieces designed in Procreate & Adobe Illustrator and laser cut using a Glowforge. The point of Meditative Mosaics is to provide a sensory experience for children who may feel stressed or overwhelmed in their current environment.
